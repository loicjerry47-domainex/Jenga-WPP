A branded client portal for freelancers and agencies. Give clients a private dashboard to track projects, submit support tickets, access documents, and communicate — all within your WordPress site.

== Description ==
Jenga Client Portal turns your WordPress site into a professional client management system. It provides a sleek, dark-themed dashboard where your clients can log in, view the status of their projects, submit support tickets, and download important documents like invoices and contracts.

* Custom branded login page.
* Fully responsive client dashboard.
* Secure document uploads and downloads.
* Email notifications for new tickets and replies.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `jenga-client-portal` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Client Portal > Settings to configure your portal pages and emails.
4. Add the `[jenga_portal_dashboard]` shortcode to your Portal page and `[jenga_portal_login]` to your Login page.
